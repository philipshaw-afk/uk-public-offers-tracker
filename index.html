What's behind the tracker

For each company in an offer period, M&A Monitor maintains a full transaction record designed to support valuation and precedent analysis, including a comprehensive narrative and background to the development and progression of each deal. The database also tracks all Rule 8.3 disclosures for each offer period, allowing detailed analysis of shareholder behaviour and trading activity.

In addition to the status information shown here, the database includes (inter alia) the following data points for UK and Ireland:

  • 8.3 shareholder register, updated instantly
  • Behavioural analysis of shareholders
  • Average trading prices in 8.3s
  • Deal valuation
  • Bid premia for multiple data points, including custom data
  • Transaction multiples
  • Competing offer history
  • Offer price bumps
